About us


Surely we have all encountered small troubles in our daily lives from the fact that the bag of clothes is overloaded by many items to the loss of keys, headphones, and phones in the backpack itself. , bags that we put in a mess. With the message "More than Simplicity", Camelia devotes herself to making her products.
